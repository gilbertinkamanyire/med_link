1. Introduction

The application is aimed to simplify how students interact with university services through a mobile device. Instead of physically visiting different offices on campus, students can access announcements, submit service requests, and manage their personal profiles using the mobile application.

02. Project Scope

The Smart Campus Service Mobile Application is designed to provide a centralized digital platform where students can easily access important university services using their smartphones.

Objectives

  • To provide students with quick and convenient access to university announcements.
  • To enable students to submit service requests directly from their mobile phones.
  • To allow students to view and manage their personal profiles.

Key Features

  • User Authentication: Secure login and signup.
  • Dashboard: Main hub for campus services.
  • Announcements: Real-time university updates.
  • Service Requests: Digital submission of reports and requests.
  • Profile Management: User data control.
